About Chorcha

Chorcha is a leading Problem-Solving and Admission Preparation Platform in Bangladesh. With a focus on helping students excel, Chorcha offers comprehensive preparation tools for HSC, Admission, BCS, and other competitive exams. Our mission is to empower students to achieve their best results with state-of-the-art learning materials and a supportive online community.

Our Journey

Since our inception, Chorcha has been dedicated to enhancing the academic experience for students across Bangladesh. We've grown from a small start-up to a platform serving over 100,000 students, boasting nearly 6 million minutes of practice and over 3 million exams taken.

Why Choose Chorcha?

Our platform is designed with the user experience at its core. From unlimited online exams to a vast digital question bank, Chorcha equips students with the tools to succeed. Our leaderboard and regular model tests foster a competitive and stimulating learning environment.

Our Features

With features like quick MCQ practice, subject-wise exams, and real-time progress tracking, Chorcha is the go-to app for students striving for excellence. Our digital archive includes past exam questions and model tests, providing a robust resource for exam preparation.

Join Our Community

Download the Chorcha app today and join a thriving community of learners. Whether you're preparing for university admissions or national competitive exams, Chorcha is your partner in the pursuit of academic success.

Be a part of our success story as we continue to innovate and redefine the educational landscape in Bangladesh. Explore, learn, and conquer your exams with Chorcha!
